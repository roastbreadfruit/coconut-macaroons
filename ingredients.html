Ingredients

7 Key Ingredients

These are the most necessary items, cannot be swapped out!

[Scroll Horizontally]

2 cups shredded coconut

2 eggs (beaten)

1 1/2 cups flour

1 cup sugar (half white and half brown)

3 tsp. baking powder

1/4 cup butter

1/2 tsp. salt

  • 1/4 cup maraschino cherries
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar half white and half brown
  • 2 cups shredded coconut
  • 2 eggs (beaten)
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 3 tsp. baking powder
  • 1 tsp. cinnamon
  • 1 tsp. ginger
  • 1 tsp. nutmeg

    • These ingredients can be sourced from any local market, or Caribbean/Jamaican Grocery store. Do not subsititue baking soda, or any of the 7 key ingredients. Cinnamon and nutmeg can be subsititude but not recoommended for taste. Add maraschino cherries to taste.
