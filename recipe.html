Macaroons

Recipe

Steps!

A short and sweet run-down

  1. Cream together butter and sugar
  2. Add coconut and eggs.
  3. Sift together rest of ingredients for flavor, such as cinnamon, nutmeg, etc.
  4. Add to coconut mix.
  5. Drop by teaspoon on greased cookie sheet.
  6. Top with half maraschino cherry.
  7. Bake at 350 degrees for 12 minutes.
  8. Makes 2 1/2 dozen.
  9. If dough seems to dry, don't add any liquid or they will spread.
